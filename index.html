bio

I'm a front-end developer with 5+ years of experience with a focus on user empathy, responsive UI, and accessibility. My strengths are creative thinking, problem-solving, and communication.

I have a BA in business administration with a background in management, marketing, and graphic design. My personal hobbies range from DIY carpentry and crafts to drumming and themed backyard movie nights.

I Moved to Austin TX 7 years ago to work in music production. After two years I wanted to pivot into software development and was awarded scholarships to complete the University of Texas in Austin Coding program and General Assembly Developer program.

I landed my first job at Personify as a Front End Developer working on the agile team for their Enterprise CRM SaaS application. After 2 years, I moved to Quinstreet where I could apply my skills to a more modern stack. I was responsible for creating, testing, and maintaining over 70 one-page websites and converting them to a modern UI that was mobile-first and WCAG accessibility compatible.

Contact me to learn more about my experience and talk about new opportunities!
